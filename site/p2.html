What are Kritiks?

Kritik is the German word for "critique", but is pronounced like "critic"!! In LD, kritiks are abbreviated as "ks".

  • Type of progressive debate (“k debaters”)
  • A field of study that looks at our way of life and criticizes it (identifies issues)
  • Explains, addresses, and provides an alternative for this issue
  • Carded
  • Disad and cp in one - read as “one off”

    • Types of Ks

  • Language K: criticizes the language the aff uses
  • Epist K: criticizes the assumptions the aff makes about the world - we’re real
  • Ideology K: criticizes the philosophy the aff uses - capitalist aff
    • Postmodernism: skepticism of generalizations in society (Ex: economy and politics)
    1. Nietzsche: god is dead
    2. Baudrillard: we’re living in a simulation
    3. Nihilism: no meaning to life
    Psychoanalysis: investigation of how people think (think Freud: id, ego, superego) Nonpess mindset: criticizes the aff’s mindset without being for one group

    Parts of a K

    1. Theory of Power
      • Criticism about the world/society
      • Usually really flowery language that comes straight from the card
    2. Link: what the aff does wrong, how they cause the theory of power to happen, and carded!
    3. Impact: the problem of this mindset that the theory of power has (ex: causes indigenous genocide) but usually not extinction impacts
    4. Alternative (Alt)
      • What we should do instead to solve this issue that is presented in the theory of power
      • the counterplan, sometimes in the form of a pik (like a pic)
      • not usually material
    5. Role of the Ballot (ROTB): framework/the lens the judge should evaluate the round under
    6. Different from structural violence because applies to one group specifically

    How to Cope with Ks - FPOSTAL!!

  • 1] Framework: extinction outweighs - you HAVE to win this
  • 2] Perms
  • 3] Offense: link turns - impact turns are iffy cause the impacts are usually true
  • 4] Solvency: their alt can’t solve for their k
  • 5] Theory: specific to the k (Ex: vague alts)
  • 6] Alt fails: makes their theory of power and impacts happen more (pos. feedback loop)
  • 7] Link: the aff doesn’t have this mindset/this issue
  • Nonunique: both sides have this issue and there’s no determining factor
  • Truth testing: do they negate the resolution?
